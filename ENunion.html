LA UNIÓN

ESPAÑOL

Tati Ortiz

I have been a ceramics artisan since 2008. I grew up with the contribution, care and guidance of my parents, the teachers Macario Ortiz and María Elena López. 
With their teachings I have managed to develop an innovative art without losing the principles of the techniques originating from Paquimé, that is why my specialty in pottery pieces is with polychrome details in Black (black with black and matte colors), working them in all the colors that exist around the community where I live.

The sphere presented is a work of my creativity using 3 colors Red, black and blue. Highlighting the work in geometry with a combination of Paquimé and Mata Ortiz. Using arrows, chains, macaws, fretwork, diamonds, blades and rabbits. All in combination with the union of designs directing the pair of rabbits that are a representative symbol of the Ortiz family. The sphere of the Union is everything that implies its meaning of links with culture, traditions, art, countries and its brotherhood through art.
